
Angband-like Keyset Command Summary
===================================

  7  8  9   Use the numbers (if from a numeric keypad, you may need
   \ | /    numlock) or arrow keys to move attack, or open doors.
  4- 5 -6
   / | \    Use 5 or z to wait a turn (and search).
  1  2  3

Use shift or . followed by a direction to move continuously.  If the
direction is 5 or z, rests until healed.

Use control simultaneously with a direction key or + followed by a
key direction to interact with a square (tunnel through rubble or
walls, closes open doors, bashes closed doors, disarms floor traps,
disarms/opens chests, attacks monsters without moving).  If the
direction is your own square, it will pick up an item or use a
staircase or forge.

  y  k  u   There is an option (in game, use = followed by selecting the
   \ | /    "User interface options" to see it), to allow the use of
  h- z -l   h, j, k, l, ... for motion.  If that option is selected and
   / | \    one of those keys or its corresponding capital letter is
  b  j  n   listed below for another action, then that action is accessible

from another key, usually control and the key pressed simultaneously.
Those alternatives are flagged with "('hjkl' option)" or "('hjkl')"
in the list below.  With the 'hjkl' motion keys enabled, , is used for
running rather than ..

Other keys are (^ followed by a letter is an abbreviation for pressing and
releasing the letter key with the control key also depressed; you may also
press and release ^ and then press and release the letter key to activate
the same command in case you system intercepts the control plus key
combination and does not pass it on):

  a    Change song                     A    -
  b    -                               B    Bash a door
  c    Close a door                    C    Character description
  d    Drop an item                    D    Disarm a trap
  e    List equipped items             E    Eat some food
  f    Fire from first quiver          F    Fire from second quiver
  g    Get objects on floor            G    Ignore an item
  h    -                               H    -
  i    List contents of pack           I    Inspect an item
  j    -                               J    -
  k    Destroy an item                 K    -
  l    Look around                     L    Locate player on map
  m    Fire at nearest target          M    Display map of entire level
  n    Repeat previous command         N    -
  o    Open a door or chest            O    -
  p    Blow a horn                     P    Toggle ignoring/unignoring
  q    Quaff a potion                  Q    Kill character & quit
  r    -                               R    Rest for a period
  s    Stand still                     S    Toggle stealth mode
  t    Take off equipment              T    Dig a tunnel
  u    Use a staff                     U    Use an item
  v    Throw an item                   V    Display version info
  w    Wear/wield equipment            W    (hjkl - locate player on map)
